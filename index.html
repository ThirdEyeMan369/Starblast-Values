Starblast Values

What is Starblast Values?

Starblast Values is a quiz that attempts to assign percentages for twelve different Starblast values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from I'd go further to This sentence angers me, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the twelve values?

There are four independent axes - Gameplay, Player Type, Modes, Thought Process, Ship Type, Ethics - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

Mining (Gameplay)
Those with higher Mining scores rather enjoys the grinding aspect of the game, such as gathering lots of credits, powermining and making quick upgrades. They tend to have the Best Contributor guild in matches due to their constant mining and depositing, or, they tend to be the first to get to higher levels before most.

Fighting (Gameplay)
Those with higher Fighting scores rather enjoys the combative aspect of the game, such as getting many kills, defeating rivals and conquering games. They tend to have the Best Killer guild in matches due to their constant killing and combating.

Team (Player Type)
Those with higher Team scores rather enjoys working with other players in the game. They tend to play modes that requires teaming such as Team Mode, Nautic Series, U-Series, Alien Intrusion, etc.

Solo (Player Type)
Those with higher Solo scores rather enjoys playing by themselves and will usually avoid taking part with a team, or, they do not follow the group of players on their own team. They tend to play modes that usually has independent gameplay such as Survival Mode, Racing and Pro Deathmatch.

Vanilla (Modes)
Those with higher Vanilla scores prefers to play the primary modes in the game. They tend to focus on improvement for the primary modes and would love to make the primary modes as fair and balanced as possible.

Modding (Modes)
Those with higher Modding scores prefers to explore other modes created by the community. They tend to focus on new concepts in the game and would love to have more mods featured in game.

Strategy (Thought Process)
Those with higher Strategy score prefers to plan ahead and coordinate with a player or more. They focus on what to do for the best outcome for themself or their team.

Impulse (Thought Process)
Those with higher Impulse scores prefers to make quick decisions and use reflex. They focus on reacting fast and rushing their opponents to create pressure.

Tank (Ship Type)
Those with higher Tank scores prefers to use ships that have higher shield and mass. These players usually like to be on the front line of battles, and act as a barricade.

Mobile (Ship Type)
Those with higher Mobile score prefers to use ships that have higher speed and agility. These players usually like to keep their distance from their opponents and be able to outrun them.

Ethical (Ethics)
Those with higher Ethical scores are players that thinks about other players and is polite towards other players. They usually say good game after a battle or match, and you may find them help new players in the game.

Unlawful (Ethics)
Those with higher Unlawful scores are players that does not think about other players, and prefers to do whatever makes themselves happy. You will usually find unlawful players spam emotes and maybe even troll other players until they leave.

What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results?

In addition to matching you to the twelve values, the quiz also attempts to match you to a Starblast role.
